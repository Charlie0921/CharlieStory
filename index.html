Elementary

Kunjoong was just a Korean student with a passion for devices, often sharing his thoughts on his blog. One of his dreams was to make friends from divers background. However, he couldn't speak a single sentence in English. Suddenly, he moved to China and attend an international school for the first time, gradually became fluent in English. This is where his journey truly began.

Middle School

When he got his first Android smartphone, he loved experimenting with different open-source firmware on his device. One day, he felt inspired to build an app and joined an after-school acvitiy called "App Developer. Alongside his interest in app development, he was passionate about environmental contributions. In the Habitat for Humanity club, he helped build a well in a community in cChina that desperately need a reliable water source.

High School

After moving back to Korea, he had to adapt to a new school environment but COVID pendamic hit. Despite the challenges of virtual communication, he returned to his blog to stay connected and continue shared his thoughts about new technology. He also dedicated an hour each week to teach English to elementary stduents from migrant families at the Seongnam Migrants Center. During the college appliacation process, he reflected on his past activites and wanted to choose a major that would allow him to use technology to address socital issues.That's why he decided to pursue Computer Science.
